About the Founder: Kyle Bailey

Kyle Bailey is a dedicated and passionate rugby player currently playing 1st XV for Villagers Newton RFC in the competitive Bolands Groot Uitdaag League. His rugby journey is nothing short of inspiring.

Previously, Kyle was a standout player in the prestigious Western Province Rugby Union. During his time there, he earned multiple individual accolades, including Best Forward, Most Improved Player, and Young Player of the Year — the latter for two consecutive years. Kyle also helped lead his team, Young Standards RFC, to back-to-back league championships during his two seasons with them.

Following his success with Young Standards RFC, Kyle joined Newton RFC, where his skills and leadership were again instrumental. He helped the team reach the celebrated Top 12 Competition and secure a major win by claiming the Groot Uitdaag League title in 2024.

Kyle’s experience across competitive leagues, his deep understanding of the game, and his desire to mentor the next generation of athletes are what make Mad Rugby a truly exceptional development center.
