Site Purpose

The website serves as an informational and promotional platform for the Mad Rugby Skills Development Center. It provides visitors with an overview of the center, training programs, coaching background, and how to get in touch. The website aims to attract youth and adult rugby players looking to improve their skills through structured development programs.

Scenarios

  • What types of training programs are offered for beginners or advanced players?
  • How can I register or get in contact with the center for more information?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Green (#004225): Used for headers, footer background, and accents.
  • Gold (#FFD700): Used for subheadings, links, and highlights.

Typography

  • Oswald (Google Font): Used for headings to give a strong, sporty tone.
  • Roboto (Google Font): Used for body text for readability and clean appearance.
